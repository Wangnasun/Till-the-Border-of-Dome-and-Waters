Justin Beiber

Justin Drew Bieber is a Canadian singer. Regarded as a pop icon, he is recognized for his genre-melding artistry and global influence in modern-day popular music. In January 2010, "Baby" was released from his debut album My World 2.0. The song is an international hit. It charted at number five on the US Billboard Hot 100, peaked at number three on the Canadian Hot 100 and reached the top ten in several international markets.
